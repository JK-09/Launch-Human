    Timeline of human space travel:

  • 1961: First manned flight carrying Yuri Gagarin (USSR).
  • 1963: First woman in space is Valentina Tereshkova (USSR).
  • 1968: First piloted orbital mission of Moon (NASA).
  • 1969: First human on the Moon and first space launch from a celestial body by Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin (NASA).
  • 1971: First space station (USSR).
  • 1971: First moon car, the Lunar Rover, driven on the moon (NASA).
  • 1984: First untethered spacewalk by Bruce McCandless (NASA).
  • 1991: First British astronaut in space, Helen Sharman.
  • 1995: First Record for longest duration spaceflight (437.7 days) set by Valeri Polyakov (Russia).
  • 2000: Permanent staffing begins on International Space Station.
  • 2001: First tourist in space, Dennis Tito, which cost 20 million dollars for a week in orbit!
  • 2004: First privately funded manned spaceflight, by SpaceShipOne.
